**The Problem**
The Problem: Fragmented Campus Life Despite being hyper-connected, students suffer from information isolation. Critical academic alerts are buried in informal WhatsApp spam, creating a "Signal-to-Noise" problem. Simultaneously, a lack of a trusted internal marketplace leads to massive resource wastage, as seniors discard expensive books while juniors struggle to afford them. Finally, the "Mentorship Gap" remains wide, as formal networks like LinkedIn feel too intimidating for casual student-alumni guidance.
